Choosing the Right Time for Auspicious Beginnings

The Power of Muhurtham

In Vedic astrology, Muhurtham (Shubh Muhurat) is the selection of the most auspicious time to initiate important life events. Aligning your actions with favorable planetary energies minimizes obstacles and attracts divine blessings, ensuring success, prosperity, and harmony.

A Shubh Muhurat is carefully determined by analyzing the five major elements of the Hindu Panchangam (Calendar). These factors influence the outcome of significant events and help identify the most favorable time.

Tithi

Lunar Day

Nakshatra

Lunar Mansion / Birth Star

Yoga

Luni-Solar Period

Karana

Half of a Tithi

Vara

Weekday Influence

Astrologers also examine planetary positions and check for doshas such as Panchaka Dosha and Ekavimshati Dosha before finalizing a Muhurtham. This ensures maximum positive results and spiritual harmony.

Important Muhurthams

Marriage Muhurtham (Vivah Muhurat)

Marriage is the beginning of a sacred lifelong relationship. An auspicious wedding Muhurtham promotes marital harmony, emotional compatibility, prosperity, and long-lasting happiness.

Gruha Pravesha Muhurtham (Housewarming)

Entering a new home at the right Muhurtham helps remove negative energies and invites wealth, peace, happiness, and good health into the household.

Mundan Ceremony Muhurtham (Choolakaranam)

The Mundan ceremony is an important childhood Sanskara. Choosing an auspicious Muhurtham supports the child's health, intelligence, spiritual growth, and protection from negative influences.
