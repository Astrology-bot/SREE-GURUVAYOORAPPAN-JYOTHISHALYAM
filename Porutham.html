The Importance of Marriage Matching (Porutham)

```

By analyzing the birth stars (Nakshatras) and zodiac signs (Rasis) of the bride and groom, astrologers determine their spiritual, emotional and physical compatibility.


Marriage matching helps identify compatibility, marital harmony, prosperity, longevity, family growth and overall happiness. It is one of the most important steps before marriage in traditional Vedic astrology.

```

The 10 Essential Components of Dasa Porutham

```

1. Dina Porutham

Ensures good health, prosperity and freedom from diseases.

2. Gana Porutham

Matches the temperaments and mental compatibility of the couple.

3. Rasi Porutham

Ensures alignment of zodiac signs for family harmony and growth.

4. Sthree Dheerga Porutham

Promotes wealth, happiness and well-being of the bride.

5. Mahendra Porutham

Indicates progeny, longevity and family prosperity.

6. Yoni Porutham

Measures physical, emotional and sexual compatibility.

7. Rashyadhi Porutham

Analyzes the relationship between the ruling lords of the zodiac signs.

8. Vashya Porutham

Ensures attraction, affection and bonding between husband and wife.

9. Rajju Porutham

One of the most important factors for marital longevity and safety.

10. Vedha Porutham

Protects the couple from obstacles, conflicts and major afflictions.

```
