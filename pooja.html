Sacred Poojas for Life's Triumph

Vedic astrology teaches that many life challenges arise from planetary afflictions, ancestral karmas, evil eye influences, family doshas, or Vastu imbalances. Through authentic Poojas and Homams, divine energies are invoked to remove obstacles and bring harmony, prosperity, health, and spiritual growth.

Our experienced Acharyas recommend personalized rituals based on your horoscope, Prasnam, and spiritual requirements.

Griha Vastu Bali

Griha Vastu Bali is a sacred purification ritual performed before entering a new home. It removes negative vibrations, harmonizes Vastu energies, and invites prosperity and peace into the household.

Bhagavathi Seva

Performed before housewarming to purify and energize the property.

Ganapathi Homam

Conducted on the morning of Grihapravesham to remove obstacles.

Panchashirassu

Sacred placement of five energized metallic figures for protection.

Sthala Raksha

Protective energized plates installed around the property.
