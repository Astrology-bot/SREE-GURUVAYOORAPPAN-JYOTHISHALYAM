Understanding Jathakam

Jathakam (Birth Chart / Kundli) is a powerful graphical representation of the cosmic blueprint at the exact moment of your birth. It records the positions of planets and stars based on your date, time, and place of birth, providing valuable insights into your life's journey.

Since even a slight variation in birth time can influence predictions, mathematical accuracy is essential. A well-prepared Jathakam serves as a complete guide to understanding your personality, destiny, opportunities, and challenges.

Grahanila & Navamsham

Main and divisional charts revealing deeper planetary influences.

Sphudangal

Precise planetary longitudes and celestial positions.

Ashtavargam & Shashtavargam

Numerical strength and effectiveness of planets.

Dashaphalam & Yogaphalam

Planetary periods, special yogas and future influences.
