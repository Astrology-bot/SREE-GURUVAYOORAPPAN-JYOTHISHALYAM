The Power of Sacred Yantras

A Yantra is the physical manifestation of divine cosmic energy. In Vedic traditions, Yantras act as spiritual energy receivers that attract positive vibrations, prosperity, protection and divine grace.

Traditional Yantras are carefully hand-etched on Gold, Silver, Copper or Panchaloha and undergo extensive spiritual energization before being prescribed.
