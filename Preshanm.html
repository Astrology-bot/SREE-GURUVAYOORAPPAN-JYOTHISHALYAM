The Power of Prasnam

Prasnam (Prasna Marga) is a highly specialized branch of Vedic astrology where answers are revealed based on the exact moment a question is asked. It is one of the most profound and detailed systems of horary astrology, offering divine guidance even when a birth horoscope is unavailable.

Through the analysis of horary charts, planetary influences, omens, Kavatis (cowrie shells), Thamboolam (betel leaves), and sacred Nimithas, hidden truths can be uncovered and practical solutions can be offered.

Life Events

Guidance regarding career growth, financial stability, marriage delays, education, business matters and childbirth.

Dosha Identification

Detection of Kalasarpa Dosha, Pitru Dosha, Kudumba Dosha, Vastu Dosha and karmic obstacles.

Negative Energies

Identification of Shatru-Abhichara, evil eye effects, hidden enemies and negative spiritual influences.

Remedies & Pariharas

Effective poojas, rituals and spiritual remedies to remove obstacles and restore peace and prosperity.

Types of Prasnam

1. Ashtamangala Prasnam

One of Kerala's most sacred astrological traditions. It uses eight auspicious items including a lamp, mirror, gold, milk, fruits, yogurt, holy scripture and white cloth. This method is used for deep spiritual insight and important family or community decisions.

2. Thamboola Prasnam

Using betel leaves offered by the devotee, the astrologer reveals hidden aspects of life. It is especially useful for locating lost items, understanding uncertainties, and obtaining guidance regarding health and wealth.

3. Devaprasnam

Conducted in temples by experienced astrologers, Devaprasnam seeks to understand the divine will of the presiding deity. It helps identify issues affecting temple sanctity and recommends corrective rituals.
